jQuery fixedFollower Example

I am watching you…

This is a demonstration of my fixedFollower jQuery plugin, which enables a Yelp-style floating menu that follows you down the page as your scroll. Some may consider this an afront to the basic dignity of web users the world over, but I remain convinced such a contrivance can be put to good use.

If you scroll down the page, the eye of newt to your right should follow you.
